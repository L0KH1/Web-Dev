What We Do

 

 

 

Growth Investing

GenCap is focused on integrating geopolitical, economic, environmental, and fundamental analyses into a single coherent vision to guide portfolio tactics for the coming 24 months.

Quantitative Portfolio Management

GenCap utilizes a set of computer programs known as "Blindside Bots" each which are individually tailored to sell individual positions if certain conditions unique to that holding are detected by the program.

Research

Each quarter, our team releases a comprehensive investment outlook for the months ahead. We come to our theses by analyzing the behavior of the global macroeconomy, and logically work down to how this affects certain geographies, industries, sectors, sub-sectors, and companies. This report covers GenCap'sown holdings as well as assessing potential areas for future investment.
